Simple Steps to your New Account
1: Choose your Account Type
You will select the type of account that best suits your needs.
2: Verify your Email and phone number
We will then begin by validating your email and phone number by sending you an OTP.
3: Upload Identification documents
You will then be required to take a photo of your identification document e.g National ID Card/Passport.
4: Preference and Occupation details
You will fill in your preference details(address details, your prefered Ecobank branch) and your occupation details.
5: Selfie
You will then take a selfie image of your face.
6: Confirm & Submit
Review your information, confirm your details, and submit your application.
